# Vision

The product is a thinking workspace for high-agency operators: founders, PM leaders, researchers, COOs, strategists, and AI-native knowledge workers.

It helps users answer:

- Why did we make this decision?
- What assumptions did we make?
- What alternatives existed?
- What uncertainty remained?
- What tradeoffs were considered?
- How has our thinking evolved?

The long-term system should feel like a synthesis canvas, reasoning ledger, hypothesis lab, and memory layer for complex work.

The first MVP remains intentionally narrow:

Research -> Critique -> Comparison -> Synthesis -> Hypothesis

The product earns trust by helping users see the shape of thought, not by pretending uncertainty disappeared.
